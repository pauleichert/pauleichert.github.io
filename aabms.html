AABMS Logo

Logo Design

Project

This logo was created for the African American/Black Male Staff organization at the University of Cincinnati. which is a support network for the professional development of African American and Black male staff. As a result, they needed a logo that encapsulated those two identities. The final three presented logos were a synthesis of these two ideas through color and form; the colors being identifiers of the Pan-African flag (with the addition of the blue hue, which was added to provide more depth to the design), and the form referencing the Black Power raised fist.

Process

Beginning with paper sketches, the process for this logo was always attempting to find some way to reference the raised fist icon. The deliverable for the project was three logos, two representational and one more abstract, with the best one being chosen to represent the organization. As a result, the paper and digital sketches below explored both literally and figuratively showing the "fist." The final three logos each illustrated the fist in some way, one combining it with the graphic of "Africa," one combining it with sleek "claw" imagery, and one simply abstracting it into a clean, spherical form. Ultimately, the one combining the fist and Africa (shown below) was chosen for use by the organization.
