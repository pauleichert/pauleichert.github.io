I did these sketches in the planning phase for my portfolio. They are primarily a series of wireframe sketches that display two different concepts for displaying my projects. In addition to visuals these sketches also displayed some information for the flow of information and the type of pages that I would use on my portfolio. Ultimately, I pursued the second concept.

I did these sketches of a Nintendo Gamecube controller as part of a greater drawing project. The goal with these sketches was obtaining and displaying visual information about the object. The sketches done were quick perspective, blind contour, longer perspective, and a final analytical drawing that utilized different three line weights to show additional information.

This series of sketches were made to exemplify several different concepts. Each one sought to display a three-dimensional object in a different way: in orthographic view, perspective view, and isometric view, respectively. A priority with this series was the usage of construction lines to display the underlying process with the sketches.
